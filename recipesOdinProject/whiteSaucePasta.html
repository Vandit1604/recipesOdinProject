White Sauce Pasta Recipe

Ingredients

Pasta , Carrots , Green Capsicum , Red Capsicum , Brocolli , Butter , Chopped Garlic , Maida , Milk , Oregano , Red Chilli Flakes , Black Pepper Powder

Directions

  • Put Pasta in Boiling Water and Boil till it Becomes Mildy Soft
  • When Done. Drain The Pasta And Remove Excess Water
  • Now take The Vegetables And Chopped them in Small Cubes and put them in a pan with some salt and cook for 2-3 mins
  • Heat Butter in a pan and half tablespoon chopped garlic and sautee for 3 mins and add maida
  • stir continously after a while add milk and keep stirring it to make white pasta cream
  • Add some oregano ,red chilli flakes , black pepper powder and salt for flavour
  • on low flame add pasta , veggies to the pan in which you prepared the sauce and mix it
    Food is Ready to be Served <3
