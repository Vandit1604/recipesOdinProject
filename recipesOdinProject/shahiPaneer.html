Shahi Paneer Recipe

Ingredients

Paneer , Tomato , Onions , Cashew Nuts , Coriander Seeds , Cloves , Ginger Garlic paste , red chilli powder , coriander powder , curd , garam masala , cooking oil , kasuri methi

Directions

  • Firstly, grind cashew nuts with coriander seeds into a powder and set it aside for now
  • blanch the onions and crush the onions to a paste like state
  • add cooking oil to stove and add cloves,bay leaf ,cinnamon , green cardamom and when this begins crackling add the onion paste
  • fry until it gets brown and add ginger garlic paste turmeric powder , red chilli powder , cashew nut powder and stir fry for a minute
  • while stll frying. add tomato puree & salt and add curd. let it boil it start thickening and add garam masla , fresh cream , saffron
  • in the end add paneer and cook for few minutes on low flame
    You're Food is Ready To be Served
