Pav Bhaji Recipe

Ingredients

2 Medium Potatoes , green peas , cauliflower , carrot , onion , ginger garlic paste , 2 tomatoes , capsicum , red chilli , turmeric powder , cummin coriander powder , pav bhaji masala , lemon juice , salt , butter , oil , pav buns

Directions

  • wash the vegetables in running water and cut them into small pieces
  • place these cut vegetables in pressure cooker and cook for 2 whistles and mash them inside the pressure cooker and put them aside
  • in a pan add oil and butter and heat at medium flame. add chopped onion and ginger garlic paste and saute until onion turns translucent
  • add chopped capsicum , chopped tomato and salt to taste and saute for a minute after that Add 1Â½ teaspoons red chilli powder, 1/4 teaspoon turmeric powder, 1-teaspoon cumin-coriander powder and 1-teaspoon readymade pav bhaji masala powder and stir and cook
  • add 3 cup water and mix well and cook for a minute add all the boiled mashed vegetables from the pressure cooker and mix it well and add taste to salt and your bhaji is Ready
  • Cut the pav buns horizontally into halves. Heat tava over medium flame. Add a tablespoon of butter and place halved pav buns over it. Shallow fry both sides until light brown spots appear, it will take around 30 seconds for each side to turn light brown. Transfer to the plate. Shallow fry remaining pavs.
  • serve it now your
